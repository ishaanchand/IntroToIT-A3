1. Project manager

An experienced project manager to lead project planning, development and deployment, involving creation and roll out of a website and mobile applications

What they will do:

  • manage the project team (technical lead, software developer, web developer)
  • manage project progress, delegating tasks as required
  • lead project meetings, scrums
  • oversee project milestone delivery
  • supervise and assist in creating project documentation
  • documenting, managing, and reporting risks
  • optimising processes and team productivity

Source: DesignLab

What skills and experience they should bring:

  • organization skills
  • time management
  • risk management
  • problem-solving
  • communication and stakeholder management skills
  • leadership
  • experience with IT project delivery (highly desirable)
  • experience in software design lifecycle methodologies (highly desirable)

Qualifications:

  • Certificate VI in Project Management Practice (minimum)
  • Bachelor of Business (Management or Information Systems)
