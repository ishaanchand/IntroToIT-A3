Group reflection:

As a whole, the group has recognised that there has been an overall improvement in our levels of communication and engagement, which has occurred in spite of increased study commitments for other subjects as well as instances of team member absence due to being unwell after COVID vaccination. Fewer meetings were conducted compared to assignment two however there was a greater amount of ad-hoc communication done on the fly.

The team has also worked more diligently to achieve its work goals in a timely manner and to avoid the stress that accompanies last minute work completion and submissions. As with the last assignment the level of focus in the team tended to increase the closer we drew to submission date.

An area of opportunity for the group was closer collaboration on the design and construction of the project artefacts (website and mobile app) as well as increasing the level of feedback offered. It is possible that if more of the team were involved in the creative aspects of the project, team engagement could improved further.
