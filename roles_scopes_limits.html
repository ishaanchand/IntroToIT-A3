IT project - Roles, scopes and limits

Roles

The team has approached this assignment in similar fashion to the previous one- assignment specifications are discussed and work requirements are divided up amongst the team- with a few differences this time; Sam has opted to create the mock-up website (as he has the most experience with coding and programming), Nima is creating mock-ups for the mobile app and Julian is compiling and editing the written work for the submission.

Scopes and limits

Due to the project requiring knowledge and skills not currently possessed by the team, a mock-up is likely as far as what would be feasible, especially given time constraints. The mock-ups could include;

  • a shell website which shows the layout, information about the service, different pages accessed by the applicants and employers
  • examples of the types of data captured and information offered
  • what the app would look like and itâ€™s functions

Without having access to a database, the group has decided to create mock-ups of job listings so that we can simulate what a finished website would look like. This includes job titles, what the job entails, and the requirements set by the employer.

As the group has little experience with programming, even less with programming for mobile systems in mind, the scope of the project is reserved to a mock-up of an app via images. The mock-ups of the app will show what the app would display to the user; a login screen, a homepage that displays all the job listings available, all the vital details about a job that has been selected, a screen that shows the user that their application has been submitted successfully, as well as a method of leaving contact information for the employer.
